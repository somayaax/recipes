Pizze dip

We'll take a slice of pizza any day, but when we're looking for a hot and cheesy party dip, this pizza dip just might be the next best thing to the classic pie

Ingredients

  • 12 oz. bacon, cut into 1" pieces
  • 1 lb. Italian sausage
  • 1 (8-oz.) block cream cheese, softened
  • 2 c. shredded mozzarella, divided
  • 1 c. ricotta
  • 1 tsp. Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 375°. In a large skillet, add bacon and cook until crispy. Drain on a paper towel–lined plate.
  • To the same skillet, add sausage and cook, breaking up meat with a wooden spoon, until no longer pink.
  • In a medium bowl, combine cream cheese, 1 cup mozzarella, ricotta, Italian seasoning and garlic powder.
